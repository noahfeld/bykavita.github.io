SORRY GRANDPA HSIAO

writer | director
released 2023
12 mins - narrative (comedy, drama) - live action

Starring Valerie Yu and Mark Daugherty.

Desperate for a promotion, a Chinese American woman enlists her "perfect" brother to help exploit her grandpa's death anniversary for a workplace AAPI heritage month social takeover.

Created for the third season of Indeed's Rising Voices in partnership with Emmy® Award-winning writer, creator and actor Lena Waithe and her company Hillman Grad Productions and 271 Films. Premiered at Tribeca Film Festival 2023.
